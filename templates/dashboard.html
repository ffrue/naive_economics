Estimating Efficient Unemployment
In a 2021 paper, Michaillat and Saez introduced the concept of an efficient rate of unemployment and estimated it for the USA. This tools allows you to do the same for European countries and analyse their labor market situations.
Mapping Sector Balances
The sector balances identity provides important insights for economic analyses and policy makers. In this post, you get a brief introduction and you can create balances map for European countries with data ranging from 1995-2021.
Assess your own Monetary Union
The theory of optimal currency areas (OCA) provides some basic criteria for judging whether a group of countries should form a monetary union. This tool allows you to make a rough cost/benefit analysis for a sample of European countries.
